Our Mission

We promote independence, inclusion, and lifelong health for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our vision aligns with the DDA’s goal of creating inclusive environments with access to essential resources and opportunities.

Our Approach

We collaborate with ISCs and families to develop Individual Support Plans (ISPs) that reflect each person’s goals and strengths. Our person-centered planning ensures individuals explore all support options and build self-determination.

Qualifications & Compliance

Our staff are trained to meet DDA standards. We follow strict quality assurance protocols and maintain open communication with all stakeholders.

Thank you for your continued dedication to the individuals and families we serve. Together, we can make a meaningful impact.
